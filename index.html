About the Festival
Where tradition meets togetherness

ପଖାଳ (Pakhala) — Odisha's most cherished dish — is far more than food. It is the soul of a summer afternoon, the taste of home, and a bond that unites generations. This ancient tradition of soaking cooked rice in water, enjoyed with an array of accompaniments, is a defining symbol of Odia culture and identity.

CYSD Alumni proudly invites you to come together and celebrate ପଖାଳ ଉତ୍ସବ — an evening of nostalgia, cultural pride, warm camaraderie, and the simple joy of sharing a beloved meal. Let us reconnect, reminisce, and revel in the roots that bind us.
